Jollof Spaghetti

Description

Jollof spaghetti is a popular West African "one-pot" fusion dish that infuses tender pasta with rich, smoky, and spicy tomato-based stew, offering a faster alternative to traditional jollof rice. It is designed to deliver a savory, umami-packed flavor profile with aromatic notes from garlic, onion, thyme, and curry powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500g) thin spaghetti or pasta of choice.
  • 2 sachet tomato paste
  • 1½ cup of blended fresh tomatoes
  • 5 red bell peppers (Optional)
  • ½ sccotch bonnets
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 tsp bouillon powder (stock cubes)
  • 2-3 cups chicken stock or water

Steps

  1. Blend the fresh tomatoes, red bell peppers, scotch bonnets, and one onion until smooth.
  2. Heat vegetable oil, fry onions, then add tomato paste and cook until the red color deepens (about 5 mins).
  3. Add the blended mix, thyme, curry, and bouillon, frying until reduced and thick.
  4. Add chicken stock, bring to a boil, and add pasta.
  5. Cover and simmer on low heat until the pasta absorbs the liquid and is tender.
